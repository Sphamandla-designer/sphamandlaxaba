02 — Conversational AI

WhatsApp
Conversation AI

For millions of South Africans, WhatsApp is not a support channel — it is the primary digital platform. This project designs an AI assistant that lives where the conversation already happens, turning WhatsApp into a natural, trustworthy way to get business done.

Challenge

Meet users where they are

Standalone apps create friction and cost data. The assistant had to integrate naturally into existing WhatsApp behaviour — no downloads, no new habits, no heavy interfaces.

Approach

Conversation as the interface

Flows designed around natural language with human-in-the-loop checkpoints, progressive disclosure for complex tasks, and clear signals about when AI is acting — building trust turn by turn.

Principles

Trust, clarity, resilience

Data-conscious and connectivity-aware by design: lightweight exchanges, transparent AI behaviour, and language that reduces uncertainty instead of adding to it.
