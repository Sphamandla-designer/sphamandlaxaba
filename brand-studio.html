The practice

Brand Studio is the identity practice inside GEMIS. We give SMEs and start-ups brands that punch far above their weight — sharp marks, confident typography and systems that scale from day one.

Strategy before style

Every identity starts with positioning — who you are, who it's for, and why anyone should care. The look follows the logic.

Small budgets, big presence

We design for the size you want to become. Start-up pricing, blue-chip posture — no watered-down "starter" branding.

Systems, not stunts

You get a kit that works everywhere: files, guidelines, templates and rules your team can actually use without us.

( How we get there )

Four moves, three weeks to first reveal.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Founders, customers, competitors — a short sharp audit of where you can win.

  2. 02

    Define

    Positioning, personality and the one sentence the whole brand must prove.

  3. 03

    Design

    Marks, type, colour and motion — explored wide, then pressure-tested in the wild.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Files, guidelines and templates handed over — plus a month of on-call tweaks.
