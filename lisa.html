03 — AI-Powered Real Estate

Lisa
AI Real Estate

Buying or renting property is one of the highest-stakes decisions people make — and one of the most confusing. Lisa is an AI companion that turns complex real-estate decisions into guided, confident conversations.

Challenge

High stakes, low clarity

Listings, financing, viewings and paperwork overwhelm first-time buyers. The product had to reduce decision anxiety without hiding the complexity that matters.

Approach

A companion, not a chatbot

Lisa guides rather than answers: structured journeys with conversational checkpoints, explainable recommendations, and behavioural design that builds confidence at every step.

Principles

Trust through transparency

Every AI suggestion shows its reasoning. Every next step is clear. The interface stays calm so the decision can have the user's full attention.
